
# 🌳 Trees Destruction & Pollution Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes environmental incidents involving plant destruction and pollution across 10 states in India. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations to identify major causes of environmental damage, evaluate the extent of tree loss, and monitor recovery efforts.

The dashboard was built using **Microsoft Excel** to transform raw data into actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Environmental agencies and policymakers need a better understanding of:

* Which states are experiencing the highest environmental damage?
* What are the major causes of plant destruction?
* How many trees are being destroyed due to pollution incidents?
* How quickly are authorities responding to incidents?
* What factors are associated with higher environmental damage?

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
The dataset is taken from kaggle datasets.
The dataset contains **3,000 environmental incident records** with the following information:

* State and District
* Incident Date
* Pollution Type
* Pollution Level (PPM)
* Trees Destroyed
* Area Affected (Hectares)
* Cause of Incident
* Recovery Status
* Damage Estimate (INR)
* Wildlife Impact
* River and Lake Pollution Status
* Government Response Time
* Rainfall
* Soil Erosion
* Air Quality Index (AQI)
* Water PH Level
* Green Cover Loss Percentage
* Industrial Area nearby Status
* Protected Area Status

## 🔍 Key Questions Answered

1. Which states have the highest financial damage by  environmental incidents?
2. Which pollution types are causing maximum tree destruction?
3. How much area has been affected by pollution?
4. which govt is taking greater time in response to incidents?
5. which state has highest tree destruction density?
6. Does higher AQI lead to greater environmental damage?
7. Are protected areas are safe after protection by govt?
